Virtual Welcome: CEO's Introduction and Expectations for Interns at Strastan Company

WEEK 1

DAY 1

The CEO of Strastan Company initiated a Google Meet session to personally introduce himself and welcome the intern students. He inquired about our expectations and what we hoped to learn during our journey with the company. The Assistant Dean, who recommended us for this opportunity, joined the meeting as well. After introducing ourselves, the CEO provided insights into what to expect during our time with the company and addressed the company rules. Additionally, he outlined the preparations needed for the upcoming tasks that would be assigned to us.

Below is the Google Sheets form sent by the CEO for us, the interns, to fill out. This form serves as a tool for the CEO to better understand our interests and skills, aiding in determining the appropriate field for each of us to focus on. By providing detailed responses, we can help ensure that our internship experience aligns with our individual learning goals and strengths.

DAY 2

On the second day, the CEO invited me to a platform called Slack, which we will use for communication and discussing our tasks as interns at Strastan Company. Sir Maf created a channel where I will be joining with Raymark and Bryan to monitor our tasks. As beginners on the platform, the CEO allowed us to explore Slack on that day so we can become more familiar with using the application.

DAY 3

On my third day as an OJT at Strastan Company, Sir gave me access to the Yundo website for my training in the field of digital marketing. This includes analytics. As my training ground before being assigned tasks, I need to first acquire an understanding and knowledge of how to execute it.

DAY 4

On my fourth day, I began training on Yundo and created notes to help me remember what tasks to do. After watching the video, I made it a habit to update Sir Maf and Sir Ricz daily on my progress so that they are aware of my activities every 9am and 5pm.

DAY 5

During training, I followed what is demonstrated in the video to make it easier for me to grasp and understand the purpose of the activities shown. This way, when I am given hands-on tasks, it becomes easier for me to execute them.
