Analytics Enhancement: GTM Implementation at Strastan Company

WEEK 5

DAY 1

On December 22, Sir Maf conducted a general assembly alongside Sir Ricz Sabido, Jhoebert, Bryan, and Raymark at 2 pm. Sir Maf led the discussion on the development of ongoing projects and shared insights into future project plans. During the meeting, Sir Maf encouraged everyone to study the Agile methodology in depth, as he plans to implement this framework next year. Additionally, Sir Maf made announcements regarding potential opportunities within the organization, hinting at plans to absorb team members into new roles or projects.

DAY 2

After confirming my implementation on Tour With Ease, I watched a tutorial on YouTube about setting up GA4, GTM, and Meta Business Suite to verify my setup. After watching the tutorial, I thoroughly tested all implementations and fixed issues such as the debug view not capturing necessary data and troubleshooting common problems. This process helped me ensure accurate data tracking and resolve any technical issues efficiently for optimal performance on the website.

DAY 3

Since the payment gateway for Tour with Ease was not yet completed, Sir Maf and Sir Ricz reassigned me to work on another website to ensure that time is used efficiently. I was assigned to the Organic Market PH project along with Sir Jhoebert, where my main task is to integrate the website with GA4 and Facebook Pixel.

Initially, Sir Jhoe requested my assistance in storing images and other information in Firestore Firebase. Before uploading the images, I compressed them to reduce file size and added watermarks for protection. Additionally, I resized each image individually and organized them into specific folders based on their categories, making it easier for the programmers to access the images efficiently. This process ensures that the images are optimized for the website and are easily manageable for the development team.

DAY 4

During a general meeting at Strastan Company, Sir Maf introduced the new interns and facilitated introductions between them and the team members. He then delved into details about the upcoming project, emphasizing teamwork and collaboration among team members and interns. Additionally, Sir Maf provided updates on the current status of ongoing projects, discussed pending tasks, and outlined plans for future projects, ensuring everyone was informed and aligned with the company's objectives and priorities.

DAY 5

After Sir Maf reviewed the images in the system that Sir Jhoebert and I had worked on, he expressed dissatisfaction with their quality. As a result, he arranged a meeting with me to provide guidance on using Photoshop effectively. He emphasized the importance of maintaining image quality even as we reduce file sizes.

During the meeting, Sir Maf shared tips and techniques in Photoshop to ensure that images remain clear and visually appealing after resizing. With this new knowledge, I proceeded to resize the images using Photoshop while preserving their quality. After resizing, I reuploaded the optimized images to Firestore Firebase, ensuring that they meet the required standards for the project. This process helped us maintain image quality while optimizing file sizes for better website performance.
