About Me

Hello there! Welcome to my blog! My name is Abegail A. Paulino, and I'm currently in my final year as a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at The Lewis College. I'm thrilled to share my journey with you as I embark on my on-the-job training experience.

I have a passion for technology and a drive to constantly learn and grow in the ever-evolving field of IT. That's why I am excited to be interning at Strastan Solution Corporate, where innovation and cutting-edge solutions are at the forefront of everything they do.

Strastan Solution Corporate is a dynamic company known for its forward-thinking approach to IT solutions. Through this internship, I have the incredible opportunity to immerse myself in real-world projects and gain hands-on experience in areas such as Digital Marketing and Analytics.

I believe that this OJT experience will not only complement my academic studies but also provide me with invaluable insights and skills that will shape my future career in the IT industry. I am eager to contribute my enthusiasm, creativity, and dedication to the team at Strastan Solution Corporate while also learning from experienced professionals in the field.

Join me as I navigate through this exciting chapter of my academic and professional journey. Together, let's explore the challenges, triumphs, and lessons learned along the way.
